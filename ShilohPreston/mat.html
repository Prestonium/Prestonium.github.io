About

Hey there, I'm Mat - a bit of a jack-of-all-trades with a variety of interests and skills. I'm a master chef and take pride in my ability to whip up delicious meals in the kitchen. I'm also a comic wizard and love making people laugh with my silly jokes and stories. And, while I may not be a seasoned programmer just yet, I'm constantly learning and growing in this field.

In addition to my skills and interests, I'm also a hard worker who takes pride in my work. I have a talent for painting and have painted everything from houses to dentist's offices. I'm also an expert at thrifting and love finding hidden gems in second-hand shops. But perhaps my most important skill is putting Wes to bed - even though I'm not his biological father, I love Wes very much and take pride in being a father figure to him. All in all, I'm a person who values hard work, humor, and a good meal.
