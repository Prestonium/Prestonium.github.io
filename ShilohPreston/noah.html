About

Hi there, I'm Noah, and I'm a bit of a renaissance woman with a variety of interests and skills. As a self-proclaimed weeb, I have a deep appreciation for Japanese culture and love watching anime, reading manga, and studying the language. I also have a green thumb and love taking care of my plants, which I see as a form of self-care. When I'm not busy with my plant babies, I'm taking care of my two cats, Ham and Marshal. As a cat mom, I've developed a range of skills, from administering medication to playing with toys to giving my furry friends lots of love and attention.

In addition to my hobbies and interests, I have several skills that I'm quite proud of. I have an amazing ability to recall details from movies, and I can often remember obscure facts and trivia about even the most niche films. I also have mad slappy ball skills and love playing this fun, fast-paced game with friends. And, to top it all off, I've been told that I have good hair - which is always a nice compliment to receive! While I'm a bit introverted at first, I love getting to know people and having deep, meaningful conversations. And, as an added bonus, I have two sisters named Shiloh and Noah, who are both amazing people.
