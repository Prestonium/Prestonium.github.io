About

Hi there, I'm Riah, and my life centers around my two passions: being a loving mother and a collector of oddities. As a mother, I take pride in my ability to care for and nurture my children. I have a son named Westley, who is the light of my life, and another son on the way who will be named June. While I may not be perfect, I do my best to provide my children with the love and support they need to grow and thrive.

In addition to my mothering skills, I also love collecting oddities - from vintage clothing to bizarre trinkets. I'm a bit of a prankster and love to make people laugh with my silly jokes and gags. I'm pretty knowledgeable when it comes to medicine, which has come in handy more than a few times. I'm also pretty crafty and enjoy making things with my hands. However, I must admit that I'm not the best with technology - sometimes it feels like it's conspiring against me! I'm currently dating Mat, who is an amazing person and loves my children as if they were his own. And, as a fun fact, I'm the sister of Shiloh and Noah. Thanks for taking the time to get to know me a little better!
