About

Hi there, I'm Shiloh - an artist, professional goofball, and all-around cutie! My passion for creating art is something that has been a part of me for as long as I can remember, and it's a joy to be able to share my creations with the world.

In addition to my artistic pursuits, I'm also a bit of a professional goofball - always looking to inject some fun and humor into everything I do. Whether it's through my art or just my everyday interactions, I believe that laughter is truly the best medicine.

When I'm not drawing or making people laugh, you can usually find me taking a nap or whipping up something delicious in the kitchen. Cooking is another one of my passions, and there's nothing quite like the satisfaction of creating a delicious meal from scratch.

Of course, all of these pursuits wouldn't be possible without the ability to get paid, and I'm grateful for any opportunities that come my way to support myself and my passions.

Thanks for stopping by my profile, and I hope you enjoy my art and antics as much as I do!
